Media statement

For immediate release

Monday, 10 February 2003 

 

 

Northern Wellington Voluntary Service Awards

 

 

The chairman of the Northern Wellington Voluntary Awards Service Committee, Ohariu-Belmont MP, Peter Dunne, said today that nominations for the 2003 awards will be called in late April.

 

“I urge community groups and individuals to start thinking now about suitable local volunteers who, by their unpaid and often unsung efforts, help to make northern Wellington a better place to live,” he said.

 

The awards will be announced midway through the year.
